# Overview

## The Opportunity

We're building the first **verified marketplace** for crypto influencer marketing—solving a massive trust crisis that costs billions annually while protecting investors, projects, and influencers from fraud.

**What we do:**
Verify influencer authenticity before campaigns, automate payment protection, and create permanent accountability through on-chain records.

**Who benefits:**
- **Investors** know promoted projects used verified influencers (not scammers)
- **Projects** hire only authentic influencers (not bot networks)
- **Influencers** build verifiable reputation (can't delete history)

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Crypto influencer marketing is broken:

- **Massive fraud**: Billions lost annually to influencer scams (Logan Paul, Kim Kardashian, Hawk Tuah Girl, and countless others)
- **Zero verification**: No way to check if influencers are authentic or running bot networks
- **No payment protection**: Projects and influencers face disputes with zero recourse
- **No accountability**: Failed promotions get deleted, scammers rebrand and continue

Traditional freelance platforms don't work for crypto projects. Specialized solutions don't exist.

**Result:** Market operates through informal Discord/Telegram—enabling fraud at scale.

[Read detailed problem analysis →](/docs/problem/trust-crisis)

## Our Approach

We lead with **protection** first, not technology:

### 1. Verified Analytics System ⚡ *Core Innovation*

**What it does:**
Directly verifies influencer social media accounts by pulling real data from platforms.

**How it protects:**
- **Investors:** Know projects hired verified influencers (not scammers with fake followers)
- **Projects:** Hire only authentic influencers (bot networks can't pass verification)
- **Influencers:** Prove authenticity, differentiate from fraudsters

**No screenshots, no self-reported data—only verified metrics.**

### 2. Automated Payment Protection

**What it does:**
Funds held securely until work is verified and delivered.

**How it protects:**
- **Projects:** Don't pay until campaign delivered
- **Influencers:** Guaranteed payment after review period (no disputes)
- **Both:** Automatic release—no "he-said-she-said" arguments

### 3. Permanent On-Chain Reputation

**What it does:**
Every campaign recorded permanently on blockchain.

**How it protects:**
- **Can't delete** failed promotions
- **Can't hide** scam attempts
- **Can't rebrand** without history following you
- **Transparent** track record for everyone

### 4. Supporting Any Blockchain

**What it does:**
Works with projects from Ethereum, Solana, or other major blockchains.

**Why it matters:**
Projects don't need to change chains to use our platform. We meet them where they are.
