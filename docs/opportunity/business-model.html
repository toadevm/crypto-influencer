Business Model

How We Make Money (Simple)​

We charge a small fee on each campaign. Lower than traditional platforms because automation reduces costs.

Core principle: Sustainable fees that benefit all parties, not maximum extraction.

Revenue Model​

Primary Revenue: Campaign Fees​

How it works:

  • Project creates campaign with budget
  • We charge fee (around 10-15%)
  • Influencer receives remainder
  • Fee covers platform operation

Example:

  • Campaign budget: $5,000
  • Platform fee (12%): $600
  • Influencer receives: $4,400

Why this works:

  • Projects get verified influencers
  • Influencers get payment protection
  • Platform sustainable at scale

Why Fees Are Lower​

Traditional platforms: 20-50% fees Our platform: 10-15% fees

How we keep fees low:

1. Automation Reduces Costs

  • Verification automated (not manual review)
  • Payments automated (not manual processing)
  • Matching automated (not human coordination)
  • Disputes minimized (clear agreements)

2. Volume Over Margins

  • Lower fees = more users
  • More users = more campaigns
  • More campaigns = sustainable revenue
  • Focus on long-term growth

3. No Legacy Cost Structure

  • No physical offices required
  • No large manual workforce
  • No traditional corporate overhead
  • Lean operation from day one

4. Technology Stack

  • Modern, efficient infrastructure
  • Cloud-based scaling
  • Automated workflows
  • Minimal operational costs

Unit Economics​

Single Campaign Economics​

Typical Campaign:

  • Budget: $5,000
  • Platform fee (12%): $600
  • Influencer payment: $4,400
  • Platform cost (automated): ~$50
  • Platform margin: $550 (~92% gross margin)

Why margins are high:

  • Automated operations
  • Low variable costs
  • Software-based infrastructure
  • Scales efficiently

Monthly Revenue Example​

100 campaigns/month:

  • Average campaign: $5,000
  • Total volume: $500,000
  • Platform fees (12%): $60,000
  • Operating costs: ~$15,000
  • Net revenue: $45,000/month

These are achievable numbers at modest scale.

Path to Profitability​

Phase 1: Validation (Month 1-6)​

Focus: Prove the model works

Metrics:

  • 50-100 campaigns completed
  • User feedback positive
  • Repeat customers
  • Clear value proposition

Revenue: $10K-30K/month Goal: Validation, not profitability yet

Phase 2: Growth (Month 6-12)​

Focus: Scale operations

Metrics:

  • 200-300 campaigns/month
  • Self-serve platform launched
  • Organic growth beginning
  • Unit economics proven

Revenue: $50K-100K/month Goal: Approaching break-even

Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)​

Focus: Market leadership

Metrics:

  • 500-1,000+ campaigns/month
  • Multiple revenue streams
  • Network effects evident
  • Sustainable profitability

Revenue: $150K-300K+/month Goal: Profitable, growing, sustainable

What We Accept​

For Campaigns:

  • Stablecoins (USDC, USDT, DAI)
  • Major tokens (ETH, SOL, etc.)
  • Native tokens (by arrangement)
  • Fiat (via payment processor)

Why flexible:

  • Meet users where they are
  • No forced bridging costs

Fee Structure:

  • Transparent pricing
  • No hidden costs
  • Simple to understand

Why This Model Works​

For Projects​

Value received:

  • Verified influencer authenticity
  • Payment protection
  • No coordination headaches
  • Transparent pricing

Cost: 12% vs 30-50% alternatives

ROI: Higher because paying for real reach, not fake followers

For Influencers​

Value received:

  • Guaranteed payments
  • Build verifiable reputation
  • No payment disputes

Cost: 12% of campaign (vs 0% on Discord/Telegram)

Trade-off: Worth it for payment security + reputation building
